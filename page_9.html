Financial Tabs Organization

To avoid mixing personal and work finances, FinMate offers different tabs to organize your budget clearly and conveniently.

Personal Expenses

Manage your personal spending, hobbies, and individual financial goals in one dedicated space.

Home & Family

Track household expenses, family budgets, and shared financial responsibilities seamlessly.

Work & Business

Separate business expenses, work-related costs, and revenue tracking for freelancers and entrepreneurs.

Studies

Manage education expenses, scholarships, and academic financial goals in one organized section.

The Benefits of Financial Tabs

Clear visualization of spending patterns across different life areas

Targeted budget limits for each category to prevent overspending

Separate tracking of income and expenses for tax preparation

Easy sharing of specific tabs with family members for joint budgeting
