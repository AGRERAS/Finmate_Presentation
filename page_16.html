Design Philosophy

FinMate's interface is inspired by Telegram, featuring calming colors, minimalist icons, and smooth animations for a familiar user experience.

Calming Color Palette

White, light gray, and turquoise create a calm and professional look. No harsh colors.

Rounded Cards & Soft Shadows

Rounded corners and soft shadows create a friendly, approachable feel.

Minimalist Icons

Flat/Fluent style icons that are recognizable at a glance.
